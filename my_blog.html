How to Save and Use Image Link

Having to always store images on your local in creating of your site may lead to image loss in the long run.

How? senerios might happen when your storage device might get corrupted or tempered with which may lead to loss of important pieces which where used in storing your code.
But having to store your images online gives you an extra insurrance of always getting that image provided the link to that site can be called upon.

Note: Uploading your code online helps create a safe backup for you.
